Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Stormworks Connect PRO?

Stormworks Connect PRO is an enhanced version of the Stormworks Connect application, offering additional features to enhance your Stormwork gaming experience.

What additional features are included in the PRO version?

At the moment, since the PRO version for Stormworks Connect has just been released, the only advantage is access to ALL Arduino modules in the corresponding tab. However, with each subsequent update, a new feature for Stormworks Connect PRO will be released, making it even more attractive.

Will the price of Stormworks Connect PRO remain at one dollar even after updates?

No, it won't. With every new feature added to Stormworks Connect PRO, its price will increase. However, those who purchased it previously will not need to pay extra again. Therefore, now is the best price.

How can I buy a PRO version?

You can buy a license key to activate the PRO version of the application here. The sale of keys is carried out by the well-known and reliable service Gumroad, which also generates these license keys.

How do I activate my PRO license?

To activate your PRO license, navigate to the PRO tab in the application, enter your license key, and click "Apply". If you encounter any issues, please contact support.

How many times can I use my Stormworks Connect PRO license key?

You can use your Stormworks Connect PRO license key an unlimited number of times. It is also infinite in time. However, one key can only be used in one application at a time.

Can I transfer my PRO license to another app or/and device?

Yes, you can transfer your PRO license to another app or/and device. To do this, go to the application where the license is activated and deactivate it in the PRO tab in the place where you previously activated the license. After that, launch the application on another device and use your license key there.

What should I do if I have lost access to an application/device with an activated PRO version and now I cannot deactivate it to regain access to the key?

Do not worry. Just contact the application creator for support using the form on this site above or directly at glebishshenko@gmail.com. Describe your situation, provide your license key and proof of purchase. After this, your license for the inaccessible program will be manually deactivated in the license database and you will be able to use it again.
